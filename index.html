薛晗 | Han Xue

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the College of AI, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Li Yi. My research is supported by GALBOT. I received my B.E. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University.

My research focuses on enabling humanoid robots to acquire complex real-world skills in a scalable and sustainable manner. I am particularly interested in moving beyond motion imitation toward automated generation and effective utilization of multi-source data.
